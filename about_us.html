About VTLABS Group

VTLABS Group is a pioneering technology company dedicated to revolutionizing STEM education and innovation in Africa. Founded in 2022 in Kigali, Rwanda, we specialize in designing, manufacturing, and delivering advanced training kits, custom electronic solutions, and hands-on technical programs that bridge the gap between theory and real-world application.

Our Story

From humble beginnings as a vision to provide affordable, high-quality STEM tools to local institutions, VTLABS has grown into a dynamic force driving educational transformation. Our journey began with the development of our first IoT and electronics kits in 2023, and today, we empower thousands of learners across schools, universities, and TVET centers. With a commitment to open-source innovation and sustainable manufacturing, we're expanding our impact across the continent.

Our Guiding Principles

Mission

To empower the next generation of African innovators by providing accessible, hands-on STEM education tools and training that foster creativity, problem-solving, and technical excellence.

Vision

A future where every young mind in Africa has the resources and skills to build groundbreaking technologies that solve global challenges.
